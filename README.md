# Impact of Social Media On Hate Crimes

This project was developed as part of the **"Economics of Migration"** course at École Polytechnique. It aims to replicate and extend the analysis conducted by Müller, K. and Schwarz, C. in *From hashtag to hate crime: Twitter and antiminority sentiment*.

We repliace the authors' event study and IV analysis on the effects of Twitter usage on anti-Muslim hate crimes in the context of Trump's political rise. In addition, we extend their approach to assess whether these effects differ across rural and urban areas.
